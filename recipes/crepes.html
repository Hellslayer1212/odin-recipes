Dessert Crepe Recipe

Description

  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a medium skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel.
  3. Using a serving spoon or a small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons of crepe batter onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface. Cook until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side; serve warm
  4. Optional: Fill the crepes with chocolate, whipped cream, honey, and fresh fruit of your choice
